Privacy Policy


Effective Date: 14/10/2024


Thank you for using CatGPT, a mobile application developed by DataChasky. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.


Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information from users of our App. However, our App uses Google AdMob to display personalized advertisements. AdMob may collect certain non-personal information, such as device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data, in order to provide relevant and personalized ads.


How We Use Information

The non-personal information collected by Google AdMob is used solely for the purpose of displaying relevant advertisements within the App. These advertisements help support the continued development and availability of the App.


Third-Party Services

Our App uses Google AdMob, a third-party service, to provide personalized advertising. Please review Google's Privacy Policy (link to Google's Privacy Policy) to understand how they collect, use, and share information.


Your Choices

You can control the personalized advertising experience within our App by adjusting your device's ad tracking settings. Please refer to your device's settings for more information on how to manage ad tracking preferences.


CCPA Privacy Rights (Do Not Sell My Personal Information)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information. However, as we do not directly collect personal information for sale, this right does not apply to our App.


GDPR Data Protection Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access your personal data, request corrections, and object to processing. As we do not collect personal information, these rights do not apply to our App.


Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe we may have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at contact email and we will promptly delete such information.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App, and the updated Privacy Policy will be effective as of the date indicated at the beginning of the policy.


Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at contact email.
